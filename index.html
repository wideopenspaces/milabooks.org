Joyful to Hear

Powerful emotion expressed through peaceful reflection.

Fifteen years of poetry - joyful songs, sad songs, love songs - along with dozens of Mila’s photographs taken over the past several years, grace the pages of Joyful to Hear, the author’s first published collection of his craft.

His words express tales of great emotion through the lenses of compassion and awareness, concluding even the saddest of songs with the rays of hope that represent Mila’s unique style.
